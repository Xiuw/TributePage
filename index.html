Facts about Ada Lovelace

  • Ada Lovelace is known as first computer programmer.
  • Ada Lovelace was born in London at 1815.
  • She is the daughter of Lord Byron(the famous poet).
  • Ada was tutored in mathematics and science in her early age.
  • In 1833, She met Charles Babbage(the father of computing) when she was 17, and Charles became his mentor
  • Ada Lovelace and Charles Babbage corresponded for years on Charles's engine machine, which could perform calculation.
  • Ada Lovelace wrote the world's first machine algorithm.
  • She's the first to recognized that the machine can do more than just pure calculation.
  • Her accomplishment in mathematics made her a pioneer of not only computing, but of women in science.
  • Ada Married William King, and have three children together.
  • She died of cancer in 1952 at age 36.
